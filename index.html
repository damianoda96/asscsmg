FROM THE KITCHEN !!
📅 MARCH 5, 2026 — SHOP SYSTEM IS LIVE!!
Posted by: DevTeam HUGE UPDATE!! The in-game shop is finally working in our internal builds!! Decorations, furniture, flooring tiles — and yeah, you can now buy AS MANY flooring tiles as you want. No more max quantity!! Fixed a bunch of bugs from playtesting too. Thanks everyone for the feedback!! 🙏
📅 FEB 18, 2026 — CUSTOMER PERSONALITY SYSTEM
Posted by: DevTeam Building a system where every regular has their OWN drink preferences, backstory and mood!! So like, one customer might always order an oat latte and be grumpy on Mondays. Super excited about how this is turning out!!
📅 FEB 1, 2026 — WHY WE PICKED GODOT 4
Posted by: DevTeam Tried Unity. Tried GameMaker. Landed on Godot 4 and never looking back. Free, open source, and perfect for a cozy sim like this. Full breakdown coming soon!!
