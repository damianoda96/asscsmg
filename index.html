FEATURES (REAL ONES)
☕ BREW DRINKS Espressos, lattes, cappuccinos — pulled through lovingly janky minigames. Mess up the order and watch your reputation tank in real time.
🏪 RUN YOUR SHOP Full day cycle: PREP, SERVICE, CLOSING, RESULTS. Clean messes, fix broken tables, repair busted windows. Glamorous, right?
🎨 DECORATE EVERYTHING Plants, lights, rugs, arcade cabinets — place them wherever you want. Buy unlimited flooring tiles because we're not monsters.
📈 EXPAND OR DIE Upgrade your lease to get a bigger shop. Miss rent and get evicted. Work recovery shifts at Dumb Starbucks until you can reopen. The American Dream.
🐀 BASEMENT DUNGEON Your shop has a basement. It's full of rats and bugs. Grab a broom and clear it floor by floor. Find golden mugs and ancient coffee grinders as loot. Normal coffee shop stuff.
🌆 EXPLORE THE STREET Step outside during prep to visit the plant shop, thrift store, and that one competitor that's definitely not a Starbucks. Name your shop whatever you want — nobody can stop you.
👤 CHARACTER CREATION Pick your hair, skin, eyes, beard, outfit. Choose from 8 hairstyles including mohawk and bald. Name yourself. Name your shop. Live your truth.
🎲 RANDOM EVENTS Health inspectors. Celebrity customers who tip huge. Equipment breakdowns mid-rush. Weather that affects how many people show up. Fun.

DEV BLOG !!
📅 MARCH 8, 2026 — WE ADDED A DUNGEON UNDER THE COFFEE SHOP
Posted by: DevTeam (questionable decisions dept.) So we were building a nice, wholesome coffee shop sim and someone said "what if there was a basement full of rats." Long story short, there's now a full roguelike dungeon under your cafe. Procedurally generated rooms, broom-based combat, loot chests, and floor progression gated by your lease tier. You can find golden mugs and ancient coffee grinders down there. We don't know what we are anymore but we're committed.
📅 MARCH 5, 2026 — SHOP NAMING + CHARACTER CREATION
Posted by: DevTeam You can now name yourself and your shop during character creation. Also added the ability to rename your shop later for $100, because apparently capitalism applies to signage too. Fixed the cement floor situation — grey and clean now instead of that busy brown tile nightmare. Also removed quantity limits on flooring because restricting floor tiles is a crime against interior design.
📅 FEB 18, 2026 — CUSTOMER AI THAT ACTUALLY WORKS (MOSTLY)
Posted by: DevTeam Customers now walk in through the door, navigate to seats, order drinks, get impatient if you take too long, and leave messes on tables for you to clean up. Just like real life. They also have speech bubbles and will visibly judge you. The satisfaction system tracks order quality, wait time, and whether you even remembered to steam the milk.
📅 FEB 1, 2026 — WHY WE PICKED GODOT 4
Posted by: DevTeam Tried Unity (licensing drama). Tried GameMaker (not enough pain). Landed on Godot 4 — free, open source, and the GDScript syntax hits that sweet spot between "readable" and "what do you mean there's no semicolons." Using GL Compatibility renderer because we're targeting potato hardware. All sprites are generated via Python/Pillow scripts because hand-drawing pixel art is for people with talent.
